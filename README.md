# Clasroyale Replica

A fan made mini Clash Royale clone that runs entirely in the browser. One HTML file, no dependencies, no build step, no server.

## Play

Open `index.html` in any modern browser, or play the hosted version via GitHub Pages once enabled.

All characters are original artwork drawn and animated procedurally on the canvas, walk cycles, attack swings, fire breath, muzzle flashes, no image assets and no extracted game files.

## How it works

You and an AI opponent each defend two princess towers and a king tower. Deploy troops on your half of the arena, manage your elixir, and destroy more towers than the enemy before the 3 minute clock runs out.

- Elixir regenerates over time, doubles in the last minute and in overtime
- 4 card hand with card cycling and a next card preview, click a card or press 1 to 4, then click the arena to deploy
- Two lanes with bridges, ground troops path across them, flying troops ignore the river
- King tower sleeps until it takes damage or a princess tower falls
- Tied at the end of regulation goes to a 60 second sudden death overtime

## Cards

| Card | Cost | Role |
|------|------|------|
| Knight | 3 | Mid HP melee fighter |
| Archers | 3 | Pair of ranged units, hits air |
| Giant | 5 | High HP tank, targets buildings only |
| Mini PEKKA | 4 | Huge single hit melee damage |
| Musketeer | 4 | Long range, hits air |
| Skeletons | 1 | Three cheap swarm units |
| Baby Dragon | 4 | Flying ranged attacker |
| Fireball | 4 | Area damage spell, reduced damage to towers |
